    B1 and B2 Interview

    August 23, 2024

    Preparations#

    • Required docs
      • any document that will prove your profile. Income proofs, job proofs address proofs.

    Mindset#

    Your interview is all about convincing the officer about your purpose and plan to visit the country. Make sure to be ready for the cross questions and have printed proofs of all your documents. Dress well and formally.

    Process#

    • Go inside
    • Peform fingerprint checking
    • Give the interview

    Precautions#

    • Don’t stutter
    • You can change interview language at the time at fingerpriting as well.
